    What is Oldschool Runescape?

    Oldschool Runescape is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed and published by Jagex. The game is based on the 2007 version of RuneScape but with improvements and new content added since then. The game can be played on both desktop and mobile platforms.

    Introduction

    Character Creation

    When you first start the game, you will be prompted to create your character. You can choose from a variety of different hairstyles, facial hair, skin tones, and clothing. You can also choose your character's name.

    Tutorial Island

    Complete Tutorial Island to learn the core mechanics of the game. You will learn about navigation, quests, and trading. Each step will add to your experience points and teach you valuable skills.

    Basic Skills

    Before jumping into quests and exploration, learn the basics of Melee, Ranged and Magic combat. Practice on low-level monsters to gain experience and level up.

    Quest Journal

    Open the quest journal and start following the free-to-play quests. Each quest offers unique challenges and rewards, and will take you on adventures throughout the game world.

    Gameplay Mechanics

    Combat System

    The combat system in Oldschool RuneScape is simple yet engaging. Players have multiple combat styles to choose from, such as melee, range, and magic. There are hundreds of monsters to fight, each with its strengths and weaknesses.

    Skills

    Oldschool Runescape has 23 unique skills to train, from Woodcutting to Fletching to Herblore. Each skill has its purpose and can be trained in various ways. The higher the skill level, the more rewards and opportunities for new content become available.

    Economy

    The game has a thriving in-game economy, with players buying and selling goods with each other. From raw materials like logs and ores to finished products like weapons and armor, the economy is entirely player-driven.

    Exploring the Gameworld

    Exploration

    The game world is vast and varied, with many different regions to discover. From the bustling cities of Varrock and Falador to the desolate wastelands of the Wilderness, there's always something new to explore.

    Socializing

    The game has a strong sense of community, with players interacting with each other through chat, clans, and events. The game hosts regular in-game events, such as seasonal events or competitive PVP tournaments.

    Cosmetics

    The game has a vast array of cosmetic items for players to customize their characters. From armor to hats to weapons, players can create a unique look for themselves within the game world.

    Stay Safe

    Beware of dangerous creatures and enemy players who could attack you. If in doubt, teleport to a bank and use the chat platform to ask for help or join a clan.

    Community Involvement

    The community is a vital part of Oldschool RuneScape. There are many ways for players to interact and engage with each other. The game offers a chat system, clans, and forums where players can discuss game-related topics, share their experiences, and make new friends. The game developers also listen to the community's feedback and suggestions and regularly update the game with new content.

    Questing in Oldschool Runescape

    Embark on epic quests with unique storylines and varied difficulty levels. Gain experience points, items, and gold as you journey through the gameworld. Keep track of your progress with the quest journal and show off your achievements to other players. Discover new areas and uncover hidden treasures as you continue your adventure in Oldschool Runescape.

    Skills and progression

    Combat

    Combat is the skill of fighting against monsters and other players. You can use a variety of weapons, armor, and strategies to defeat your opponents. As you level up, you will unlock more powerful gear and abilities. Combat is a challenging and rewarding skill that can be trained in many different ways.

    Magic

    Learn and use spells that will buff, debuff, protect, or even attack enemies. Every spell requires different items, runes, and resources to cast and can be tricky to use at first. Magic can be a powerful skill that can help you in combat, but it can also be used for other purposes like teleporting and enchanting items.

    Prayer

    Train your prayer skill by offering bones and ashes to altars. Prayer can be used to heal yourself, boost your stats, and protect yourself from enemy attacks. As you level up, you will unlock more powerful prayers and more effective ways to train the skill.

    Crafting

    Use raw materials to create bows, jewelry, clothes, potions, and more. Crafting can be profitable, as you can sell your items to other players or use them to improve your character's stats.

    Fishing

    Catch fish, and other aquatic creatures, to sell or cook. You can also fish in different locations with different requirements and rewards. Fishing is a relaxing and profitable skill that can be trained at any level.

    Cooking

    Cooking is the skill of preparing food and drinks. You can make simple dishes like bread and cheese, or complex ones like pies and stews. Cooking can be profitable, as you can sell your food to other players or use it to heal yourself. As you level up, you will unlock more recipes and more efficient ways to train the skill.
